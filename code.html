Exploring Memories

Go Explore

The goal for this project was to use still images as a constraint in the blending of non-digital and digital media. My group and I decided to achieve this by remediating the telling of a story into a video game, specifically using old photos as the unifying element. Our project describes a hypothetical grandfather"s experiences in World War II with photographs, and then remediates these photographs into a virtual world allowing the user to peruse them and consume the story at will.

After the completion of this project, a paper was written discussing the theoretical motivations behind our implementation, the process used, and possible directions the project could have continued in. To read more, click here.
